About

Hello ! I'm Mathias De Magalhaes! Holding a Master's degree in Innovation Management with a major in design, I believe that design is a real way of improving the daily lives of the people I work for. I particularly enjoy the user research phases and guiding the mapping of the feelings and needs of the users I interview. Generally speaking, I opt for a highly visual approach in collaboration with the stakeholders in order to map out all the elements needed to keep the project on track.
